Why Invest

  • Lowest entry price among finalists — R$5–7k/m² beachfront, with room to appreciate.
  • Natal has 10.82 homicides/100k — the lowest of any capital in the Northeast, near WHO's "tolerable" threshold.
  • Established European tourist base with long-stay demand — consistent year-round rental income.
  • Dramatic cliffs, surf, kite, dolphins, gastronomia — strong aspirational vibe internationally known.
  • Duplicated highway from Natal's new airport simplifies access from NAT.
  • Tibau do Sul (neighboring area) offers even lower entry prices vs Pipa proper — better upside.

Risks to Consider

Appreciation (6–10%/yr) is lower than João Pessoa. Pipa proper is already well-established, which limits upside compared to emerging areas in Tibau do Sul. RN state outside tourist areas still has elevated crime — stay coastal.
