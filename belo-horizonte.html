Why Invest

  • #1 in Latin America for startup ecosystem growth (Dealroom Rising Stars 2025) — #4 globally. +34.9% growth, $72M in tracked funding.
  • Hotmart HQ (unicorn, operating in 180+ countries) based in BH. FIRE 2025 event injected R$70M into the city. Growing cluster: Solides, Blip.ai, Sympla.
  • CNF (Confins) has direct flights to Lisbon, Orlando, Santiago — 67 total destinations across 7 countries. Easy international reach.
  • Savassi: R$18,053/m2 avg (+13.2% YoY) — among Brazil's fastest appreciating premium neighborhoods. City avg +12% for 2 consecutive years.
  • Food capital of Brazil — highest quality of life among major Brazilian cities. Cost of living ~13% below Brasília, far below São Paulo/Rio.
  • UFMG (Universidade Federal de MG) — strong research output feeding the tech ecosystem. Strong mining/energy investment network (Vale, Petrobras ecosystem).

Risks to Consider

Numbeo crime index: 63/100 ("high") — risk is real but concentrated in peripheral areas. Premium neighborhoods (Savassi, Lourdes, Funcionários) are safe and well-policed. No beach access — purely entrepreneurship/lifestyle play. Airport is ~35–44km from center, which is farther than ideal.
